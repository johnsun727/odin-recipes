Pho Recipe


A nice steamed egg

Description

Pho is a Vietnamese soup dish consisting of broth, rice noodles, herbs, and meat – usually beef, and sometimes chicken.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Beat eggs
  2. Add water
  3. Steam for 10 minutes
  4. Add soy sauce on top
  5. Serve
